How It All Started...

You know that feeling when you awkwardly raise your hands at a restaurant, eyeing a staff’s movement like a stalker,
waiting for them to acknowledge you and hear you out? We know that feeling well. Maybe too well. After extensive
research, we noticed that restaurants lose huge potential sales due to that awkward experience. So, we decided to
develop AQLUT. AQLUT is a cutting-edge digital ordering solution, streamlining the dining experience at a ZERO%
commission. AQLUT offers a seamless digital process to help eliminate everyday restaurant hiccups while increasingv
sales.
